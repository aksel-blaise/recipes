4.1 Amy’s Hummus

From the kitchen of Amy Pangburn

  • 1/2 cup tahini
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 2 15oz cans garbanzo beans/chickpeas, drained
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

In a food processor or blender, blend tahini and olive oil. Then add other ingredients and blend well. Garnish with sprinkle of salt and swirl of olive oil.
